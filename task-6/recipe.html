The Art of the Perfect Burger

INCREDIENTS

  • Maida
  • Oil
  • Vegetables
  • Salt
  • Chilli Powder and Seasonings
  • Sauce
  • Cooking Oil

STEPS

  1. Prepare the dough using maida, salt, and water.
  2. Cut vegetables like onion, tomato, and lettuce.
  3. Heat oil and cook the patty until golden brown.
  4. Toast the burger buns lightly.
  5. Apply sauce on both sides of the bun.
  6. Add vegetables and cooked patty inside.
  7. Close the bun and serve hot 🍔
