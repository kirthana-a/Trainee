My Favourite Place- Marina Beach

Why I Love It

My favourite place is the marina beach. I love the sound of the waves and the feeling of sand between my toes. The beach is a great place to relax and unwind, and I always feel happy when I'm there.

Beautiful Veiw of Marina Beach

Things I like

  • Watching the sunrise and sunset
  • Collecting seashells
  • Enjoying street food from nearby vendors
  • Taking long walks along the shore

Things I dislike

  • Overcrowding during peak hours
  • Littering and pollution
  • Noise from nearby vendors
  • Occasional water pollution
